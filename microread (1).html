Why this matters

Shrinking attention spans

Scrolling culture has rewired how learners process text — passive consumption replaces active comprehension.

Declining literacy scores

Global reading benchmarks have dropped steadily since 2012 across secondary and tertiary education levels.

Equity gap in access

Learners without structured support fall furthest behind — SDG 4 demands inclusive solutions for all.

Visual background Mode 01

User-selected visual background reading

Interactive game-style reading

Reading content is embedded into step-by-step interactive tasks. You must engage with each prompt to unlock the next — making comprehension an active, game-like experience from start to finish.
